Making AI

I'm a software engineer who specializes in one kind of problem: making AI that can hold a real conversation — and then do something with it.

Currently building outbound voice AI at GroundGame.Health in Tampa while working as an AI Software Engineer at UMass Amherst's Manning College. My systems have handled over 50,000 real automated interactions — in English and Spanish.

I also build things on my own time. Mindry is a voice-first AI that turns messy spoken thoughts into structured goals and action plans — running 100% locally via Ollama, zero data leaving your machine.

Before this I worked across the stack: security tooling at Avocado Systems, e-commerce at VYBN, Azure healthcare platforms at Hitachi Solutions. I've also published two peer-reviewed papers and led 4,000 students as club president.

I care about one thing: systems that hold up in production — not just demos.
